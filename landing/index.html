FAQ

Q:Are beauty products tested on animals?

Ans: Many beauty brands are now cruelty-free and do not test their products on animals. Look for the "cruelty-free" or "not tested on animals" labels.

Q: Can you recommend some cruelty-free beauty brands?

Ans: Some popular cruelty-free beauty brands include Tarte, Urban Decay, Too Faced, and Anastasia Beverly Hills. There are many more options available, so explore and find what works for you.

Q: How do I properly apply makeup for a natural look?

Ans: Start with a lightweight foundation or BB cream, apply a touch of blush, define your eyebrows, add a coat of mascara, and finish with a nude lip color for a natural and effortless look.
